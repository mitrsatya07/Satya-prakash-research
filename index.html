Research Topic

Comparative Study of Early Historic Ceramics from the Dhundhar Region

Abstract: This research investigates Early Historic ceramic assemblages from Dhundhar sites—such as Nagar, Tonk, and Galta—through typological and compositional analyses. It aims to identify cultural continuities and trade linkages using GIS mapping.

Objectives:

  • Typology of ceramics across Dhundhar sites
  • Inter-site cultural comparison and chronology
  • Reconstructing settlement patterns and ancient trade
  • GIS-based spatial distribution mapping
